Small Steps


Making a difference to the planet, one action at a time.

Step 2 - Home

There are many small adjustments you can make to your home in order to reduce its carbon footprint, and minimise energy use while still remaining comfortable:
  • Conduct an energy audit of your home - this can show you how energy may be overused or wasted, and help identify ways to be more energy efficient. Make sure your house is properly insulated to minimise the amount of heat you need
  • Change incandescent lightbulbs to LED lightbolbs - these use a quarter of the energy, and last up to 25 times longer than old-style lightbulbs
  • Switch lights off when you leave the room and unplug your electronic devices when they are not in use
  • Turn your central heating down and wear more layers in colder weather
  • Avoid using air conditioning in the summer/hot weather, instead optimising your home to maximise cooling and airflow
  • Sign up to a clean or renewable energy provider for your utilities
  • Invest in your own sources of renewable energy, for example, by putting solar panels on the roof
